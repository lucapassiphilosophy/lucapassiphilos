About

I'm a PhD student in Philosophy at UC Berkeley. My interests are mainly in moral theory. I also have secondary interests in philosophy of language and normative decision theory.

Before coming to Berkeley, I studied in Pavia (BA), London, and Cambridge (MPhil).
